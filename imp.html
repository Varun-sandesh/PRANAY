About Us

 Welcome to "OURSPACE" – Your Companion for Mental Wellness 
        
                At "OURSPACE", we believe that mental health is just as important as physical health. 
        Our mission:
        To break the stigma around mental health and provide a safe, supportive,
        and confidential space for everyone seeking help.
       
        
        Our Vision: 
        To create a society where mental health is prioritized, understood, and accessible to all.
